Biology 101: Cellular Fundamentals

1. The Cell Theory
All living organisms are composed of cells. The cell is the basic unit of life. Cells arise from pre-existing cells.

2. Organelles
- Nucleus: The control center containing DNA.
- Mitochondria: The powerhouse of the cell, generating ATP via cellular respiration.
- Ribosomes: The sites of protein synthesis.
- Chloroplasts: Found in plants, these conduct photosynthesis to convert huge light energy into sugar.

3. Cellular Processes
- Photosynthesis: 6CO2 + 6H2O + Light -> C6H12O6 + 6O2
- Respiration: C6H12O6 + 6O2 -> 6CO2 + 6H2O + ATP
- Mitosis: Cell division resulting in two identical daughter cells.
